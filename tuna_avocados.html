Tuna Stuff Avocado

Description

There is a one more healthy, easy, delicious recipe! We use tuna and avocado in this recipe and both of them are rich of protein and healthy fat.

Let's start!

Ingredients

  • 4 avocados

  • 70gr cans tuna

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

  • 1 stalk of celery

  • 2 tablespoon red onion

  • 1-2 tablespoon chopped parsley

  • 1/2 tablespoon mustard

  • salt and paper

Steps

  1. Add the tuna, mayonnaise, diced celery, diced red onion, herbs, mustard, salt and pepper to a mixing bowl. Stir together until well combined.

  2. Slice the avocados in half and remove the seed. Dollop a few spoonfuls of tuna salad onto each avocado half.
